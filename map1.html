Hoosier Choropleth Map

The Hoosier Choropleth map shows the states with a higher occurrence of the term in their newspapers and allows for comparisons between the states. Indiana has the highest percentage of pages containing the term hoosier, followed by Arkansas, Kentucky, Iowa, Minnesota, and Connecticut. To use this map, hover over the state to see the hoosier numbers for each state. Click on each state to see the search results for hoosier in Chronicling America in a new tab. Zoom the map using your trackpad or your mouse.
